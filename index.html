About Us

Lilyk Food, Spice and Event Management is a trusted name in food manufacturing and event planning, proudly serving the Bwari Area Council and beyond from our base in Kubwa Phase 4. We combine traditional Nigerian flavors with modern manufacturing standards to produce premium food products and authentic spices that enhance every meal. Our event management service ensures your special occasions – from weddings to corporate gatherings – are executed flawlessly. With years of experience and a commitment to quality, we've become the go-to choice for families and businesses who refuse to compromise on excellence.

Our Services

Food Manufacturing

We produce a wide range of quality food products using the finest ingredients and maintaining the highest hygiene standards. Our manufacturing process preserves the authentic taste you love while ensuring safety and consistency.

Premium Spices

Our spice collection includes ground pepper, curry powder, thyme, ginger, garlic powder, and traditional Nigerian spice blends. Each product is carefully processed to retain maximum flavor and aroma, bringing restaurant-quality taste to your home kitchen.

Event Management

From intimate family gatherings to large corporate events, we handle every detail with professionalism. Our services include venue decoration, catering coordination, vendor management, and complete event execution. We specialize in Nigerian celebrations including weddings, naming ceremonies, birthday parties, and business launches.
